Project: Show Us What the May 2024 Geomagnetic Storms Looked Like

Project Summary

This project visualizes the solar flares and geomagnetic storms of May 2024 using NASA data and AI simulations. It aims to educate the public about the effects of solar storms on Earth.

Detailed Project Description

This project includes visualizations of solar activities, including images and animations of solar flares and auroras. It uses real-time data from NASA and NOAA to provide accurate information about geomagnetic storms.
